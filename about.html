About Us

The Student Government Association is a student-run club at York County Community College. We focus on improving the school experience and acting as a student voice for the administration and staff.

Our Meetings

In the SGA we use Robert Rules of Order during our meetings to have efficient and effective meetings where officers work together to come up with and report on current tasks. We have four officer positions, the president, vice president, secretary, treasurer and other members as well. Our current elections are going to take place at the end of the school year, so there are officers at the start of the next year. Our meeting times are decided at the start of each semester and are generally take place between 12 and 1. The meetings are roughly an hour from the start time. If you are interested in sitting in or joining us, we would love to have you! Feel free to contact us or come to the Hussey conference room in the Pratt and Whitney building.

Meet the Officers
