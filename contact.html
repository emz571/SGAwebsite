Talk with Us!

In the SGA we welcome anyone who wants to join us, chat with us, or give us insight on school life. We try to take the information students and staff give us and work toward the betterment of student life. We would love to hear your comments, concerns and ideas you may have for us. As of right now, the SGA meets at 12:30pm on Tuesdays in the Hussey conference room on the second floor of the Pratt and Whittney building. If you are interested in joining feel free to stop by or email our advisor Allyson Grochmal at agrochmal@mainecc.edu for meeting times, events and club member information.
